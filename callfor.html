CALL FOR PAPERS

SPECIAL ISSUE: WOMEN IN SCIENCE IN CONTEMPORARY AFRICA

The ASFI Research Journal (ASFIRJ) announces a special issue to be published in its June 2025 issue under the theme “Women in Science in Contemporary Africa”

This special issue will highlight the voices and contributions of African women scientists from a historical, cultural, and multidisciplinary perspective. It aims to collect original research papers, expert reviews, perspective papers, and other paper types that explore and highlight the situation and contributions of African women in science to the development of the continent.

Issue Editors:

Dr. Eunice Fondze-Fombele:

  • Associate Professor of African Literatures and Cultural Studies, University of Buea, Cameroon
  • Associate Editor, ASFI Research Journal
Dr. Eman Sobh:
  • Associate Professor of Respiratory Diseases, Al-Azhar University, Cairo, Egypt
  • Editor, ASFI Research Journal
Dr. Lara Ferrero Gómez:
  • Associate Professor of Biological Sciences, Jean Piaget University of Cabo Verde
  • Associate Editor, ASFI Research Journal

Dates to Note:

State 1: Abstract Submission: 15th December 2024
Stage 2: Full Manuscript Submission: 31st March 2025
Special Issue Publication Date: 30th June 2025

Publication Fee:

All papers accepted for this special issue will be published free of charge.
